FlyPREMIER

FlyPREMIER fares are available on all Firefly destinations. Get additional 10kg on top of the 20kg baggage allowance. Enjoy the flexibility of free 1x date or time change of your flights up to two (2) hours prior to the scheduled flight departure.

FlyPREMIER can be booked online at www.fireflyz.com.my.​

FlyPREMIER benefits includes

  • 30kg checked baggage allowance
  • Free seat selection (Preferred & Desired Seats are subject to availability)
  • Priority baggage
  • One (1) free Flight Change up to two (2) hours to scheduled time of departure. Waiver of change fee and fare differences may apply.
  • Earn BonusLink points
  • Peugeot Lounge Access (only available in Subang Skypark Terminal)
